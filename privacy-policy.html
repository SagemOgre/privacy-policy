My Media Pro

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

Overview

My Media Pro is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All your files and recordings stay on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how the My Media Pro application ("App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it on your Android device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your media files, audio recordings, or playlists
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Crash reports sent to our servers

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Audio recordings you create using the built-in recorder
  • Playlists and playback preferences you configure
  • Downloaded video files (Pro feature)
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is fully under your control. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI (Audio Transcription)

The Pro version includes an AI-powered audio transcription feature. If you choose to use this feature, audio data is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. This requires you to provide your own OpenAI API key in the App settings. We do not have access to your API key or any audio sent to OpenAI. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

Google Play Billing

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

YouTube

The video download feature (Pro) interacts with publicly available online content. We do not log or store URLs or any data related to your downloads.

Permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions:

  • Storage / Media access — to read and play your local audio and video files
  • Microphone — to record audio using the built-in recorder
  • Internet — required for transcription (OpenAI API) and video downloading features only

Permissions are used solely for the features described above and never for tracking or advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

My Media Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
